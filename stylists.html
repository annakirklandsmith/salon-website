Stylists

TALIA

Owner and Stylist

Talia believes how we carry ourselves, especially our fashion and hair is our most profound expression. Our personality, confidence, and spirit can all be seen in our style. She believes in establishing crystal clear understanding and a vision is key in the relationship between stylist and client.

During her 18 years of hair artistry, Talia has been involved in many hair shows, in Baltimore, Washington D.C. and Raleigh, and has worked in top salons within those cities. ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀

Talia is sensitive to personalizing each clients cut and, or color to compliment their lifestyle, individuality and personal taste. To her, there is nothing better then empowering the women in her community with bad ass hair!

Beyond the salon she enjoys, concerts, traveling, and discovering new places with her two beautiful daughters.

CLEO

Stylist

The moment you sit in her chair, you feel comfortable and at ease. Cleo believes that every person who sits in her chair is unique and their hair should reflect their individuality.

She specializes in balayage along with fun and textured haircuts. Cleo loves to tell people she was born and raised in Raleigh, which is true, and rare. She did get the amazing opportunity to live and work in a top salon in London for a year.

When Cleo is not behind the chair she spends her time with her beautiful one year old daughter, husband, and many cats.

If you get the chance to hear one of her colorful and humorous stories, you won’t forget it.
